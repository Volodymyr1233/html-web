Playing saxophone

Saxophone is my life. When I play I feel better and it helps me reduce my stress.
I have been playing the saxophone since I was 10 years old. My saxophone is Alto Sax.
It is a medium size instrument with a curved neck and bell-shaped mouthpiece.
Most beginners start out playing this type of sax because it produces a mellow tone that is easy to play.
Professional musicians also use Alto Saxophones for their versatility and ability to blend in with other instruments.
My best song is Despacito
