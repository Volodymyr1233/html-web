Card games

There are many different card games but my favourite are:
Blitz:
A popular and casual/social card game, also known as "Scat", "Thirty-One", "Ride the Bus", and "Blitz".
By drawing and discarding a card each turn, the aim is to try to improve your three card hand to have the closest to 31 points in one suit.
Cheat:
Also called "I Doubt It" or "Bullsh**", this is a game many children have played.
The aim is to be the first to get rid of all your cards, and you can bluff about
what cards you are playing on a turn, but if you get challenged and
caught out you have to pick up the entire pile.

I play when I have time :)
